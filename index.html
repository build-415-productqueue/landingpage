Lambda Labs simulates an apprenticeship experience at a development shop. Students work on real-life projects from businesses like yours on a pro bono basis.

Labs projects are production-ready applications that showcase industry standard techniques such as authentication, database modelling, payment processing, and more. Lambda students are fully capable of bringing your dream application to life.

Lambda students' efforts are partly graded on their professionalism. Due to this, Labs projects' design and UX are often indistinguishable from tech created by large firms or cutting-edge startups.

By submitting a Labs project, you'll get to see how students will solve the types of problems your business deals with every day. We can even help tailor the stack our students use to your company via Lambda Custom.
